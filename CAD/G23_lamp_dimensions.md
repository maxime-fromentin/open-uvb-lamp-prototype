# G23 Lamp Dimensions

This note collects approximate dimensions used to model the 9 W G23 lamp for the project.

## Known or Standard Reference Values

- Maximum overall length: about 6.5 in = 165.1 mm, often rounded to 165-167 mm.
- Power: 9 W.
- Base: G23, 2 pins.

## Approximate Values to Validate

- Tube diameter: about 11 to 12 mm, T4-style tube.
- Pin spacing: about 7.8 mm, G23 standard.
- Pin diameter: about 0.9 to 1.0 mm.
- Visible pin length: about 6 to 8 mm.
- Maximum width, tube outside to tube outside: about 28 to 32 mm.
- Plastic base: about 31 x 15.5 x 16 mm.
- Base shoulder or neck: about 27 x 13 x 6 mm.

## Measurements Needed for Final CAD

1. Exact overall length from pin tip to top of U-bend.
2. Exact maximum width from outside tube to outside tube.
3. Exact tube diameter.
4. Exact pin spacing and pin diameter.
5. Exact depth and height of the G23 base block.
6. U-bend height and radius.

## Note

The approximate values above are suitable for a first 3D mockup, but final geometry should be validated against the real lamp or an exact manufacturer datasheet.
