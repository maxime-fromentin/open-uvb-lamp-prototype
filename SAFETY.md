# Safety Notes

## Your Responsibilities

- **Competence:** do not reproduce or modify this build unless you have real electrical and mechanical competence for mains-powered UV equipment.
- **Shielding:** the enclosure must be fully opaque to UV radiation.
- **Eye protection:** use certified UV protective eyewear, for example EN 170 goggles marked `2C-1.2` or equivalent. Never look at the bare lamp, even briefly.
- **Ventilation:** UV lamps heat up, and their output spectrum can vary with temperature. Provide airflow if the lamp is enclosed.
- **Mains safety:** protect the live conductor with a fuse, bond accessible metal parts to protective earth, and keep mains wiring physically separated from low-voltage electronics.
- **Measurement:** do not rely on assumed dose. Measure UVB irradiance and, ideally, the emitted spectrum before any exposure.

This repository documents a prototype. It does not make the device medically or electrically safe by itself, and it does not replace professional review or local compliance obligations.
